Kamal Acharya

Kamal Acharya (कमल आचार्य; born 27 August 1998) is a Nepalese researcher and author in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT).

Early Life and Education

Acharya was born in Kailali district of Nepal. He completed his SLC from Janapriya Secondary School (2014), intermediate from Radiant Secondary School (2014–2016), Bachelor in CSIT from Farwestern University (2016–2019), and Master in CSIT from Tribhuvan University (2019–2022).
