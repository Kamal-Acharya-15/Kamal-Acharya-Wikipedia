Kamal Acharya (कमल आचार्य; born 27 August 1998) is a researcher and author from Nepal who works in the field of computer science and information technology (CSIT). He is interested in many areas including project management, science and technology, computer engineering, writing research articles, and using tools like Google Analytics to understand data[2]. Kamal studies how technology can be used to solve real-life problems and develops projects and software systems for schools, businesses, and other organizations. He has published several academic papers and project reports that show his skills in applying knowledge to practical work. He is active on platforms like SSRN, ResearchGate, ORCID, and Google Scholar, where he shares his research and connects with other researchers. His work helps improve understanding of technology and inspires students and professionals in Nepal and beyond. [3]

Early life and education

‎Kamal Acharya was born on August 27, 1998, in the Kailali district of Nepal, where he was raised in a supportive family by his parents, Dilli Raj Acharya and Sharada Devi Acharya,[4] who encouraged his academic growth from an early age; he completed his early schooling at Janapriya Secondary School in Kailali, earning his School Leaving Certificate (SLC) in 2014 with a strong focus on science subjects, after which he pursued his Intermediate of Science (I.Sc.) at Radiant Secondary School in Mahendranagar from 2014 to 2016, further strengthening his foundation in scientific and technical studies, and later continued his higher education by enrolling in a Bachelor’s program in Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT) under Farwestern University and Master's program in Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT) under Tribhuvan University, where he developed deeper knowledge in computing, programming, and information systems, shaping his academic and professional direction in the field of technology. [5]
